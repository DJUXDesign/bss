Funeral Expenses Payment

Potentially eligible

The caller may be eligible, but at the moment we only take details if they were in a relationship with the deceased.

Use an electronic form (SF200) if the caller wants to apply.

If the caller lives in Scotland

They need to apply for Funeral Support Payment (FSP) to get help with funeral costs.

You should transfer them at the end of the call, or if they prefer, they can apply online at www.mygov.scot or call 0800 182 2222.
