Next steps

You should register the death if you haven’t already.

You can check if you can get any financial help by:

  • visiting your local jobcentre for advice on what benefits you could be entitled to
  • using an independent benefits calculator at www.gov.uk/benefits-calculators
  • contacting your local council to see if you can get help to pay Council Tax

Citizens Advice can also give you support and guidance.

Help with funeral cost

If you need help to pay for a funeral you’re arranging, you may be eligible for a Funeral Payment. You can apply now over the phone.

You can also download an application form from www.nidirect.gov.uk or get a copy from your local Jobs and Benefit Office.


Emotional support

You can contact Cruse Bereavement Care, a voluntary organisation, for emotional help and support during a bereavement.

Telephone: 0808 808 1677

Email: helpline@cruse.org.uk
