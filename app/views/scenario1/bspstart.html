Bereavement Support Payment

Potentially eligible

The caller could be eligible because they’re the spouse or civil partner of the person who died.

To claim, they must have been under State Pension age when their partner died.

What they could get

They could get:

  • a £2500 lump sum payment
  • up to 18 monthly payments of £100

The payments could be higher if they have dependent children.

The money is tax free and paid directly into their bank account.

Bereavement Support Payment will not affect any other benefits for a year after the first payment.

! Warning You will need to do a security check on the claimant
